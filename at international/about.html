About A.T. International

12+ years of leather craftsmanship excellence

Our Story

A.T. International was founded by Mr. Toufique Ahmad with a vision to create genuine leather products that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern quality standards. Based in Kanpur, India's leather capital, we have been serving customers worldwide for over 12 years.

Our Expertise

We specialize in manufacturing authentic leather belts, bags, and dog accessories. Our skilled craftsmen use traditional techniques combined with modern tools to create products that are both durable and stylish.

Quality Commitment

Every product undergoes careful inspection to ensure it meets our standards. We use only genuine leather and authentic materials to create products that last.

Mr. Toufique Ahmad - Founder

With over 12 years of experience in leather manufacturing, Mr. Ahmad has built A.T. International into a trusted name in the leather industry. His commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives our business forward.
